About Us

Get To Know Our Owner!

In 2006 John Johnson bought a fledgling pizza place in Indianapolis, Indiana called Sassy Sauce Pizzeria. John always had a love for pizza and decided it was time to open up his own Pizzeria. Since then Sassy Sauce Pizzeria has become a beloved pizzeria in the Downtown of Indianapolis. At Sassy Sauce Pizzeria there is something for everyone to enjoy! Come on in and let us know what you're craving!

Our Mission

It is our goal to handcraft every pizza with the highest quality ingredients from our family to yours.

How We Do It

We do what we say we are going to do when we say we are going to do it. We earn the right to hold others to a higher level of accountability by being accountable to ourselves, our customers and our business partners. We never stop trying to surpass our previous best. We constantly "Raise the Bar." No matter how good we are, we will always get better. Our customer satisfaction must be consistent, quantifiable and demonstrable. At Sassy Sauce Pizzeria we expect excellence - the "best in its class" in everything we do.
